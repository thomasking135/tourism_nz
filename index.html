Frequently Asked Questions

Got some questions? Here are some of the most common ones we receive

  • What budgets do your accommodation cater to?

    All sorts! That includes those coming here on a stroestring budget to those looking to indulge in some high quality accommodation.

  • What numbers do your accommodations cater for?

    Our accommodation caters for single to up to four people.

  • What are the average prices for your accommodation?

    That depends on the type of accommodation. The prices range from $30 up to $240 per person per night .

  • What is the minimum and maximum time which you can stay in your accommodations?

    This comes down to the accommodation type and the number of guests. The minimum stay typically ranges from 1 day up to a maximum of 15 days per person and/or group.

  • Do you guys offer any other services?

    We sure do! You can arrange meals including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. You can also ask for add ons including room service, cable, internet access, and even pool and gym access.
